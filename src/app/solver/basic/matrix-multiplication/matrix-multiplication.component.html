Matrix Multiplication

Let A = [ a i j ] be m x p and B = [ b i j ] be p x n matrix. Then the product of the matrices A and B, denoted as A * B (or simply AB), is the mxn matrix whose (i,j)th entry for 1 ≤ i ≤ m and 1 ≤ j ≤ n, is the entry of the product of the ith row of A and the jth column of B; more specifically, the (i,j)th entry of AB is:

a i 1 b 1 j + a i 2 b 2 j + ... + a i p b p j
